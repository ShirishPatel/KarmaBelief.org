About Us

Our Story

Karma Belief Charitable Trust was founded on 9th July 2019 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, with a simple yet powerful belief: "Service to humanity is service to God."

What started as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a community-driven organization working across multiple social, environmental, and educational domains. We are proud to be a 100% volunteer-run NGO, where every member contributes their time, skills, and compassion to create meaningful change.

Our roots are in Ahmedabad, but our impact extends across Gujarat and beyond. From tree plantation drives to scholarship distributions, from women empowerment workshops to old age home visits — every initiative reflects our commitment to building a more compassionate and inclusive society.

"We believe that every small effort counts, and together, we can make a lasting impact."

Timeline

  • July 2019 — Trust founded and registered
  • 2019 — NITI Aayog NGO Darpan registration
  • 2021 — Section 12AB & 80G certification
  • 2024 — 100+ active volunteers, countless lives touched

Our Work Areas

We work across four key domains to create lasting impact

🌱 Environment & Sustainability +

We organize tree plantation drives, river and lake cleaning campaigns, and environmental awareness programs. Our goal is to protect and preserve our natural resources for future generations while promoting sustainable living practices in our communities.

👩 Women Empowerment +

We provide scholarships for girl students, conduct skill development workshops, and support programs that promote financial independence. Our motto: "Empowering Education, Empowering Womanhood." We believe that when women rise, society rises.

💍 Marriage Assistance +

We support underprivileged families with dignified marriage ceremonies, ensuring that financial constraints don't prevent families from celebrating this important milestone with respect and joy.

🎓 Scholarship & Education +

We distribute scholarships to underprivileged students, provide study materials for school children, and encourage higher education especially for girls. Education is the foundation of progress, and we're committed to making it accessible to all.
