Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and how you can help

About the Organisation

What is Karma Belief Charitable Trust? +

Karma Belief Charitable Trust is a volunteer-driven non-profit organization founded on 9th July 2019 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We work across education, environmental conservation, women empowerment, and community welfare with the belief that "Service to humanity is service to God."

When was Karma Belief established? +

Karma Belief Charitable Trust was established on 9th July 2019 and is registered under the Public Trust Act with registration number E/22616/AHMEDABAD.

Where is Karma Belief based? +

We are based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Our registered address is B/806 Ashima Tower, Near Sandesh Press, Bodakdev, Ahmedabad – 380054, Gujarat, India.

Is Karma Belief a government-recognized NGO? +

Yes, we are officially registered and recognized by the Government of India. We are registered with NITI Aayog NGO Darpan (ID: GJ/2019/0240259) and hold certifications under Section 12AB and Section 80G(5)(iv) of the Income Tax Act.

Donations

How can I donate to Karma Belief Charitable Trust? +

You can donate via bank transfer to our Equitas Small Finance Bank account:

Account Name: Karma Belief Charitable Trust
Account Number: 100056019687
IFSC Code: ESFB0007023
Bank: Equitas Small Finance Bank, Prahladnagar Branch, Ahmedabad

Is my donation eligible for tax deduction? +

Yes! Donations to Karma Belief Charitable Trust are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G(5)(iv) of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Our PAN is AAETK0087F. You will receive an 80G certificate for your donation.

How will I receive my 80G certificate? +

After making your donation, please email your bank transfer receipt to info@karmabelief.org along with your name, address, and PAN details. We will send you the 80G certificate via email within 7-10 business days.

Is there a minimum donation amount? +

No, there is no minimum donation amount. Every rupee counts and makes a difference. Whether you donate ₹100 or ₹10,000, your contribution directly impacts lives in our community.

Can I donate in-kind (food, books, clothes)? +

Yes! We accept in-kind donations such as books, study materials, food, clothes, and other resources. Please contact us at info@karmabelief.org or call +91 9886596789 to arrange pickup or delivery.

Is online donation available? +

Currently, we accept donations via bank transfer. Online payment gateway integration is coming soon. For immediate donations, please use the bank transfer details provided on our Donate page. Contact us if you need assistance.

Volunteering

How can I become a volunteer? +

We welcome passionate individuals who want to make a difference! Please contact us via our Contact page or email info@karmabelief.org with your details and areas of interest. We'll get in touch with you about upcoming volunteer opportunities.

Do I need any special skills to volunteer? +

No special skills are required! We have opportunities for everyone. Whether you can help with fieldwork, social media, event planning, teaching, or simply want to contribute your time and energy, there's a place for you in our volunteer team.

Can organizations/companies volunteer as a group? +

Absolutely! We welcome corporate volunteer groups and CSR partnerships. Group volunteering is a great team-building activity while making a positive social impact. Contact us to discuss customized volunteer programs for your organization.

Our Work

What areas does Karma Belief work in? +

We work across four key areas:

  • 🌱 Environment & Sustainability (tree plantation, river clean-up)
  • 👩 Women Empowerment (scholarships, skill development)
  • 💍 Marriage Assistance (supporting underprivileged families)
  • 🎓 Scholarship & Education (study materials, merit support)
Do you work outside Ahmedabad? +

While we are based in Ahmedabad, our activities extend across Gujarat. We are open to expanding our reach based on resources and partnerships. If you'd like to collaborate on a project in your area, please contact us.

How can I see your past activities? +

Visit our Gallery page to see photos from our tree plantation drives, scholarship distributions, women empowerment workshops, community events, and more. You can also follow us on Facebook at facebook.com/karmabeliefcharitabletrust for regular updates.
