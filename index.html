Who We Are

Karma Belief Charitable Trust is a volunteer-driven non-profit organization founded on 9th July 2019 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We are a community of passionate individuals united by a single belief: Service to humanity is service to God.

Our work spans across education, environmental conservation, women empowerment, and community welfare. Every initiative we undertake is powered by the dedication of our volunteers and the generosity of our supporters.

We are officially registered and recognized by NITI Aayog, with certifications under Section 12AB and 80G, ensuring transparency and tax benefits for our donors.
